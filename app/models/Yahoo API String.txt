SYMBOL can be singular or plural (eg: AAPL+T+GOOG)

s	=	symbol
n	=	name
l1	=	last trade price
c1	=	change (price)
p2	=	change (percentage)
j1	=	market cap
o	=	open price
h	=	daily high
g	=	daily low
v	=	volume
a2	=	average volume
r	=	P/E ratio
k	=	52 week high
j	=	52 week low
y	=	yield
